Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Usage

At WebSim, we prioritize your privacy and data security. We collect only essential information needed to provide our simulation services:

  • Basic account information
  • Simulation configurations and preferences
  • Usage analytics to improve our platform

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for all simulations
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Strict access controls and authentication

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data deletion
  • Opt-out of non-essential data collection
  • Export your simulation data
